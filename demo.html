Latest Projects

Face Recognition

Develop a face recognition system is a technology that identifies individuals by analyzing unique facial features, for authentication application in bank industry. It detects faces in images or video frames, extracts distinctive facial characteristics, converts them into numerical representations, and matches them against a database of known individuals. If a match is found, the system confirms the person's identity.

Face Anti-Spoofing System

Develop a face anti-spoofing system to detect and prevent fraudulent attempts to deceive facial recognition systems using fake or manipulated images, videos, or 3D models. This system employ various techniques, such as analyzing liveness indicators like eye blinks, skin texture changes, to differentiate between genuine faces and spoofing attacks. By identifying and rejecting fraudulent attempts, face anti-spoofing systems enhance the security and reliability of facial recognition applications, safeguarding against unauthorized access and identity theft.

Image Quality Assessment

Developed a Image quality assessment process to evaluate the visual fidelity and perceptual quality of images to determine their overall image quality. It encompasses the assessment of factors like sharpness, color accuracy, contrast, noise, and artifacts, typically performed by comparing the original image with a processed or compressed version. Various objective metrics and algorithms, as well as subjective evaluations involving human observers, are employed to quantify and rate image quality, to ensure that images meet desired standards and user expectations.

Chatbot

Develop a chatbot for general information for an enterprise as a AI-driven virtual assistant designed to provide customers a quick and accurate responses to a wide range of inquiries and requests related to the organization. It leverages natural language processing and a repository of company-specific knowledge to answer questions about products, services, policies, processes, and other relevant topics. By offering real-time assistance and information retrieval, this chatbots enhance user satisfaction, streamline communication, and improve overall efficiency within the enterprise. It was deployed to web-ui and telegram channels.
